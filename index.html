IX · The Hermit — Questions

Before You Book

Do I need to prepare a question?

It helps to have something on your mind, but it's not required. Some of the best readings begin with nothing more than "I'm not sure where I'm headed." Devyani will help you find the question underneath.

Is this in person or online?

Both. Choose an in-person sitting or a video call when you book — the reading is just as clear either way. Online sessions are held over a private video link sent to you after booking.

How do payments work?

You pay securely when you reserve your time, through Stripe or PayPal. Your card details never touch this website — they're handled entirely by the payment provider.

What's your rescheduling policy?

Life happens. You can reschedule up to 24 hours before your reading at no charge using the link in your confirmation email.

Is everything confidential?

Completely. What's shared in a reading stays in the reading, full stop.

Can tarot predict my future?

Think of a reading as a mirror, not a crystal ball. The cards illuminate the patterns and choices in front of you — what you do with that clarity is always yours.
